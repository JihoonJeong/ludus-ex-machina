# Case Brief: The Double Alibi

## Incident Summary

On the morning of March 22, 2026, at approximately 7:10 AM, Mr. Jung Taeyang (45), CEO of Hangang Development Corp., was found dead in the outdoor hot tub at Pine Valley Pension in Gapyeong, Gyeonggi Province.

Mr. Jung was attending an annual reunion dinner with four college friends. The group of five arrived at the pension on the afternoon of March 21 and had dinner and drinks through the evening. Mr. Jung was last seen alive at approximately 12:30 AM on March 22, at which point the group was winding down their drinking session in the main living area.

The victim was discovered by Ms. Choi Eunji, who woke early for a morning walk. He was submerged in the hot tub, which was still running. The hot tub is located on the rear deck, partially visible from the living room but not from any bedroom.

## Initial Assessment

Local police initially classified the death as accidental drowning — likely intoxication leading to unconsciousness in the hot tub. However, the Gapyeong medical examiner flagged the toxicology results as inconsistent with simple alcohol-related drowning and referred the case to the Gyeonggi Provincial Police major crimes unit.

## Suspects

The four surviving members of the reunion group:

- **Suspect A (Ms. Choi Eunji):** Attorney at Kim & Chang. The victim's girlfriend during college (2001-2003). They broke up acrimoniously but reconciled as friends years later. She discovered the body.

- **Suspect B (Mr. Kim Hyunwoo):** Managing Director at Mirae Asset Securities. The victim's business partner in two real estate development projects. Their business relationship has been described by mutual acquaintances as "complicated."

- **Suspect C (Ms. Seo Yuna):** Pharmacist, owns a pharmacy in Bundang. The quietest member of the group. Has attended every reunion for the past 18 years without fail.

- **Suspect D (Mr. Park Taehyun):** Architect at Gensler Seoul. Known as the "social glue" of the group. Organized this year's reunion.

**Note:** A sixth friend, Mr. Lee Joonho, cancelled two days before the reunion due to a family emergency (hospitalized mother). He was in Busan at the time and is not considered a suspect.
