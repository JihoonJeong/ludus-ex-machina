# Alibi — Suspect C (Ms. Seo Yuna)

**Statement summary:** Retired to Room 1 (upstairs) at approximately 11:30 PM — earliest of the group. Said she was tired from the 2-hour drive from Bundang. Read a book on her Kindle for about 30 minutes, then slept. Woke to screaming at approximately 7:00 AM.

**Verification:**
- Room assignment confirmed: Room 1, upstairs, window facing rear (toward deck/hot tub area). **Note: From this window, the hot tub area is partially visible.**
- Phone activity: Last phone usage at 11:28 PM (KakaoTalk message to pharmacy employee: "I'll be back Monday afternoon. Please handle morning prescriptions.") No activity until 7:08 AM.
- Kindle: Amazon account records show reading activity on Kindle device until **11:55 PM** (last page turn in "The Vegetarian" by Han Kang). Consistent with her statement.
- **Key observation:** Ms. Seo stated she went to bed at 11:30 PM. Other witnesses (Choi, Kim, Park) all confirm she left the main gathering around 11:30 PM. However, **no witness saw her enter her room.** The staircase to the upstairs rooms is not visible from the living room.
- Room condition: Bed slept in. Room notably tidy. Toiletry bag organized.
- Ms. Seo appeared calm when interviewed. She cried briefly when asked about the victim but composed herself quickly.

**Assessment: Alibi consistent with available evidence but entirely self-reported with no independent corroboration. Ms. Seo was alone from 11:30 PM onwards with no witness placing her in her room during the critical window (1:00–3:00 AM). The Kindle data confirms reading until 11:55 PM but does not prove she remained in the room after that.**
