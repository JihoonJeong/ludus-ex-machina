# Newspaper Archive — Recovered During Investigation

The following article was found during a background check on all suspects and the victim. It was retrieved from the Bundang Ilbo digital archive.

---

## Bundang Ilbo, August 18, 2006

### "College Student Killed in Late-Night Hit-and-Run; Driver Identified but Released"

A 17-year-old high school student, **Seo Junhyeok**, was struck and killed by a vehicle in Bundang on the night of August 15, 2006. The driver, identified as **Jung Taeyang** (25), a graduate student at Korea University, was apprehended at the scene.

According to police, Mr. Jung was driving home after a gathering with friends. His blood alcohol level was measured at **0.11%**, above the legal limit. The victim was crossing the street near Seohyeon Station when Mr. Jung's vehicle struck him at approximately 11:40 PM. The victim died at Bundang Seoul National University Hospital at 1:15 AM on August 16.

Despite the clear DUI circumstances, prosecutors **dropped the charges** after reaching a settlement with the victim's family. The settlement amount was not disclosed but sources indicated it was "substantial." Mr. Jung's attorney argued that the victim was crossing outside of a designated crosswalk and that the intersection had poor lighting.

**The victim's family — parents Seo Changho (then 52) and Lee Misook (then 48), and elder sister Seo Yuna (then 21)** — reportedly agreed to the settlement under financial duress, as the father's small business was failing at the time.

Local civic groups criticized the outcome as an example of "checkbook justice," noting that Mr. Jung's family owns a large construction company.

## Follow-up Article — Bundang Ilbo, August 25, 2006

### "Victim's Father Appeals to Prosecutors; Motion Denied"

Mr. Seo Changho, father of the late Seo Junhyeok, filed an appeal with the Suwon District Prosecutor's Office requesting the case be reopened. The appeal was denied. Mr. Seo told reporters: "My son is dead because a rich kid drove drunk, and the system tells me to take the money and be quiet. This is not justice."

The victim's sister, **Seo Yuna** (21, pharmacy student at Sungkyunkwan University), was briefly quoted: **"I will never forget what happened to Junhyeok. Never."**

---

**Investigator's note:** Ms. Seo Yuna (Suspect C) is the elder sister of the victim in this 2006 case. The drunk driver was Mr. Jung Taeyang — the current victim. **Ms. Seo has known the identity of her brother's killer for 20 years.** Despite this, she has attended the annual reunion (which includes Mr. Jung) every year since 2008. She has never publicly discussed the accident or her connection to it. None of the other reunion members were aware of this history — when interviewed, all three expressed shock.
