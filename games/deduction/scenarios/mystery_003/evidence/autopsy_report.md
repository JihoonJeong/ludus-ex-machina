# Autopsy Report — Victim: Mr. Jung Taeyang

**Gapyeong Medical Examiner's Office, Case #2026-GP-0322**

## Cause of Death
**Drowning.** Water consistent with hot tub water found in lungs. Cause of death is freshwater drowning.

## External Examination
- Male, 45 years old, 178cm, 82kg
- No defensive wounds or bruising
- No signs of struggle on skin or under fingernails
- Minor abrasion on right knee (consistent with bumping edge of hot tub)
- Skin wrinkling on hands and feet consistent with prolonged water immersion (estimated 5-6 hours)

## Internal Examination
- Lungs: bilateral pulmonary edema, water in airways
- Heart: mild left ventricular hypertrophy (consistent with known hypertension)
- Liver: mild fatty changes (consistent with regular alcohol consumption)
- Stomach contents: partially digested food (meat, rice), approximately 3-4 hours post-meal. Alcohol detected.
- No evidence of physical restraint or compression injuries

## Blood Alcohol
- BAC at time of death: estimated **0.12%** (moderately intoxicated but not incapacitating for a regular drinker of the victim's size and drinking history)

## Key Finding
- **See separate toxicology report.** A substance inconsistent with simple alcohol intoxication was detected. This finding elevated the case from accidental to suspicious.

## Estimated Time of Death
Between **1:00 AM and 3:00 AM**, March 22, 2026. Based on body temperature, water temperature, and lividity patterns.

## Medical Examiner's Opinion
The level of intoxication alone (BAC 0.12%) is **unlikely to cause unconsciousness** in a regular drinker. The victim's medical records indicate a high alcohol tolerance (regular social drinker, 3-4 times per week). Combined with toxicology findings, this death is **inconsistent with simple accidental drowning** and warrants investigation as a potential homicide.
