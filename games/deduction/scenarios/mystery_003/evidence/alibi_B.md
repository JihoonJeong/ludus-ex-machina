# Alibi — Suspect B (Mr. Kim Hyunwoo)

**Statement summary:** Went to Room 3 (downstairs, adjacent to deck) at approximately 12:20 AM. Could hear hot tub jets running. Fell asleep. Woke to screaming at approximately 7:00 AM.

**Verification:**
- Room assignment confirmed: Room 3, ground floor, **shares a wall with the deck area**. Window faces the deck but curtains were drawn (confirmed by crime scene photos).
- Phone activity: Text messages sent to wife at 12:25 AM ("reunion going ok. taeyang still being taeyang. going to sleep. love you"). No further activity until 7:05 AM.
- **Alcohol level:** Mr. Kim's self-reported consumption: "6-7 glasses of whiskey, 3 beers." At his weight (91kg), this is consistent with significant intoxication. He stated he "passed out more than fell asleep."
- **Proximity concern:** Mr. Kim's room is the closest to the hot tub. He could have exited through his room's sliding door directly onto the deck without passing through any common area. However, the sliding door lock was found in the **locked position** from inside on the morning of March 22.
- Room condition: Bed slept in. Shoes placed inside the room. Sliding door locked.

**Assessment: Alibi plausible but unverifiable. He was alone in a room directly adjacent to the crime scene. However, no physical evidence places him outside his room during the critical window. The locked sliding door suggests he did not exit through the direct route. Motive exists (see financial_dispute.md) but physical evidence is absent.**
