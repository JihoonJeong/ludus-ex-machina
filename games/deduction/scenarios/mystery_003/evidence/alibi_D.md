# Alibi — Suspect D (Mr. Park Taehyun)

**Statement summary:** Went to Room 4 (upstairs) at approximately 12:25 AM. Left room at ~12:45 AM to retrieve phone charger from car. Passed by deck, saw victim in hot tub. Returned to room by ~12:55 AM. Slept until woken by screaming at ~7:00 AM.

**Verification:**
- Room assignment confirmed: Room 4, upstairs, window facing front (parking area side).
- Phone activity: Phone usage until 12:22 AM (Instagram browsing). **No activity from 12:22 AM to 12:50 AM** (consistent with going to car without phone, since the charger was the reason). Activity resumes at 12:51 AM (phone plugged in — battery went from 8% to charging). Usage until 1:15 AM (YouTube), then no activity until 7:02 AM.
- **Car charger retrieval:** A Samsung USB-C car charger was found plugged into Room 4's outlet on the morning of March 22. The charger had a Gensler (Mr. Park's employer) logo sticker on it. Consistent with his statement.
- **Pension exterior camera:** See pension_cctv.md. Camera captures Mr. Park walking to the parking area at 12:43 AM and returning at 12:54 AM. He passes through the partial view of the deck area both times.
- **Last person to see victim alive:** Mr. Park states he saw the victim in the hot tub at approximately 12:45-12:50 AM. The victim appeared conscious (or at minimum, eyes-closed-relaxed). This is consistent with the estimated time of death (1:00-3:00 AM) — the victim was alive when Mr. Park saw him.

**Assessment: Alibi verified by phone charging data, CCTV footage, and physical evidence (charger in room). Mr. Park was outside during a narrow window (~12:43-12:54 AM) but this was before the estimated time of death. His account is internally consistent and externally corroborated.**
