# Witness Statement — Security Guard (Mr. Bae Sangho)

I'm the night shift security guard at Hanjin Technologies headquarters. My shift starts at 8 PM.

On March 15, I did my first round at 10:00 PM. I noticed the CEO's office light was on, which wasn't unusual. At 10:30 PM, I went to check — I knocked, got no answer, and used the master key because the door was deadbolted.

Regarding visitor logs: the building has badge access. I checked the logs for the evening:

- Mr. Han Dongwoo (PA): Badge out at **5:32 PM**. Did not return.
- Ms. Kang Soyeon (VP): Badge out at **6:00 PM**. Did not return.
- **Mr. Yoon Jaemin: Badge out at 6:20 PM. Badge IN again at 8:45 PM. Badge out at 9:15 PM.**

I was surprised to see Mr. Yoon came back in the evening. When I reviewed the lobby camera, I could confirm he entered at 8:45 PM and went to the elevator. He came back down and left at 9:15 PM. He was **not carrying anything** when he arrived or left.

**Investigator's note:** Mr. Yoon's alibi states he left at 6:20 PM and went straight home. He did not mention returning to the building at 8:45 PM.
