# Witness Statement — Cleaning Staff (Ms. Lim Sunhee)

I am part of the evening cleaning crew for the Hanjin Technologies building. My shift runs from 7:00 PM to 11:00 PM.

On March 15, I was cleaning the 15th floor (executive offices) starting around **8:50 PM**. I heard the elevator ding and saw **Mr. Yoon Jaemin** step out. I recognized him — he's a frequent presence on the executive floor even though his own office is on the 12th floor.

He didn't seem to notice me. He walked directly to Mr. Choi's office and tried the door. It was locked — I could tell because he jiggled the handle. He then **bent down near the bottom of the door** for about 30 seconds. I thought it was strange but assumed he was sliding a note or document under the door. I heard a faint **click** — like a lock engaging.

He straightened up, looked around (he noticed me then and nodded), and walked back to the elevator. He didn't say anything to me. The whole thing took maybe two minutes.

I continued cleaning and did not approach the CEO's office. The lights inside were on but I could not see through the frosted glass.

**Investigator's note:** The office door has a mail slot at the bottom. Forensic testing confirmed that the deadbolt knob can be reached and turned through the mail slot using a long, thin tool (a ruler or similar object). This explains how the door was deadbolted from outside while appearing to be locked from inside.
