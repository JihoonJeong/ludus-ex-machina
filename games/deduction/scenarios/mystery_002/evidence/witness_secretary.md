# Witness Statement — Secretary (Ms. Jang Eunhye)

I am the executive secretary for the C-suite floor. On March 15, I was at my desk until 6:30 PM.

At approximately **5:55 PM**, I saw Mr. Yoon Jaemin walking toward Mr. Choi's office carrying a **tea tray** — a porcelain cup on a small tray. I thought it was unusual because Mr. Yoon doesn't normally bring tea; that's usually Mr. Han's job or mine. Mr. Yoon said, "Daeho asked me to bring him tea. We're going to have a talk." He entered Mr. Choi's office and closed the door.

Mr. Yoon came out about **20 minutes later**, around 6:15 PM. He seemed normal — not agitated or hurried. He said "goodnight" to me as he passed my desk.

I left at 6:30 PM. Mr. Choi's office light was still on, which was not unusual — he often worked late.

I did not see anyone else enter Mr. Choi's office after Mr. Yoon left.
