# Case Brief: The Silent Partner

## Incident Summary

On the evening of March 15, 2026, Mr. Choi Daeho (58), CEO of Hanjin Technologies, was found dead in his office at the company headquarters in Pangyo. The building security guard discovered the body at approximately 10:30 PM during a routine check after noticing the office lights still on.

The victim was slumped over his desk. A cup of tea was on the desk, half-consumed. The office door was locked from the inside using the deadbolt — security had to use the master key to enter.

## Initial Assessment

Cause of death is pending full forensic analysis. No signs of struggle. No forced entry. The locked door presents a puzzle — the office has no windows that open and no other exit. However, the deadbolt can be engaged by turning the knob from inside, meaning someone could have locked it on their way out if they had a way to turn the knob after closing the door (e.g., reaching through the mail slot, or the victim locked it himself before losing consciousness).

## Suspects

- **Suspect A (Ms. Kang Soyeon):** VP of Operations. Has been with the company for 12 years. Was passed over for the CEO position when the board chose to keep Mr. Choi despite her strong candidacy.

- **Suspect B (Mr. Yoon Jaemin):** Co-founder and minority shareholder (30%). Has been in a bitter dispute with Mr. Choi over a proposed buyout of his shares. The two reportedly had a heated argument in the boardroom on March 13.

- **Suspect C (Mr. Han Dongwoo):** Personal assistant to the CEO for the past 4 years. Was the last known person to see the victim alive, departing the office at approximately 5:30 PM.
