# Witness Statement — Security Guard (Mr. Oh Junghwan)

**Date:** March 11, 2026, 10:00 AM
**Location:** Samcheong-dong Police Box

I work as a night security guard for the Samcheong-dong commercial district. My patrol route includes the block where Gallery Maru is located. I walk past the gallery approximately every 90 minutes.

On the night of March 10, at approximately **11:50 PM**, I was walking past the rear alley behind Gallery Maru. I noticed the emergency exit door closing — it made a distinct clicking sound. I saw a figure walking quickly toward the main road. The person was wearing a dark coat and carrying what appeared to be a **large rolled-up tube or cylinder**, about 1 meter long.

The figure was **male, average height, thin build**. I did not see the face clearly due to distance and poor lighting in the alley. The person turned left onto the main road and I lost sight of them.

I did not think much of it at the time — people sometimes use the back alleys as shortcuts. It was only when police contacted me the next day that I realized it might be connected to the theft.
