4. **Endgame**: In K+R+P vs K+R+B, activate your king and rook to create winning chances.
5. **Study Opponent**: Use recent_moves to understand opponent's defensive patterns.

- Your king is active on f5. Use it aggressively.
- Your rook on c1 should create threats (checks, cuts).
- Opponent's king is exposed on d5. Look for forcing sequences.
